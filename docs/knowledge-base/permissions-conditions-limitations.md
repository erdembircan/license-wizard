# Permissions, Conditions, and Limitations

Every open-source license defines three dimensions: what it **permits**, what it **requires** (conditions), and what it **disclaims** (limitations). This reference table compares the most common licenses across all three.

## Permissions

What the license allows you to do with the software.

| Permission | MIT | Apache 2.0 | BSD 2-Cl | BSD 3-Cl | ISC | GPL v2 | GPL v3 | LGPL | AGPL |
|------------|-----|-----------|----------|----------|-----|--------|--------|------|------|
| Commercial use | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Modification | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Distribution | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Private use | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Patent use | — | Yes | — | — | — | — | Yes | Yes | Yes |

## Conditions

What you must do when using the software.

| Condition | MIT | Apache 2.0 | BSD 2-Cl | BSD 3-Cl | ISC | GPL v2 | GPL v3 | LGPL | AGPL |
|-----------|-----|-----------|----------|----------|-----|--------|--------|------|------|
| Include copyright | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Include license | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| State changes | — | Yes | — | — | — | — | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Disclose source | — | — | — | — | — | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Same license | — | — | — | — | — | Yes | Yes | Yes* | Yes |
| Include NOTICE | — | Yes | — | — | — | — | — | — | — |
| Network use is distribution | — | — | — | — | — | — | — | — | Yes |

*LGPL: same license applies to modifications of the library itself, not to the linking application.

## Limitations

What the license disclaims or does not grant.

| Limitation | MIT | Apache 2.0 | BSD 2-Cl | BSD 3-Cl | ISC | GPL v2 | GPL v3 | LGPL | AGPL |
|------------|-----|-----------|----------|----------|-----|--------|--------|------|------|
| No liability | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| No warranty | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| No trademark rights | — | Yes | — | — | — | — | — | — | — |
| No endorsement | — | — | — | Yes | — | — | — | — | — |

## Quick Classification

| License | Category | One-Line Summary |
|---------|----------|-----------------|
| MIT | Permissive | Do anything, keep the copyright notice |
| Apache 2.0 | Permissive | Like MIT, plus patent grant and NOTICE file |
| BSD 2-Clause | Permissive | Minimal conditions, no patent grant |
| BSD 3-Clause | Permissive | BSD 2-Clause plus no-endorsement clause |
| ISC | Permissive | MIT-equivalent with shorter text |
| GPL v2 | Copyleft | Derivatives must be GPL v2 |
| GPL v3 | Copyleft | GPL v2 plus patent grant and anti-tivoization |
| LGPL | Weak Copyleft | Copyleft for the library, not for linking code |
| AGPL | Strong Copyleft | GPL v3 plus network use counts as distribution |
